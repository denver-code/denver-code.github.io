Noto - No Tomorrow.

Description Noto (No Tomorrow) - this is a simple, minimalist application that will help you organize your workflow. Here you can add tasks, change them, change their status, delete them, assign them to be completed on a particular day, or simply to a continuous list of functions, or you can make a shopping list so that you don't forget anything in the store.

What you can do now?

  • add new tasks
  • change their checking status
  • delete tasks
